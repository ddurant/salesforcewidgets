Meetings

Meetings allows you to keep track of all your meetings in an organzied widget this is lightning ready for you to drag and drop on any page layout as needed. Meetings is customizable with the ability to customize even the meetings widget itself.

You can also add other individuals to meetings from other accounts just in case you have meetings with multiple accounts at the same time.

Agenda

Agenda is an awesome sales tool for admins! With Agenda you can create robust meeting agendas importing images from excel and any other tools you use to create sales reports.

The idea behind agenda was to have the ability to pull from reports inside salesforce to create a comprehensive dashboard with notes that you could email out to your sales team anytime you want!
